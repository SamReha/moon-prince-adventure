Moon Prince: Prince of the Moon is a classic-style adventure game set on the Moon. You play as Moon Prince, a young prince who was ripped away from his vacation on Earth when disaster struck. Called back to the Moon, you must explore, collect ancient treasures and ultimately, defeat evil and restore order to the Moon Kingdom!
